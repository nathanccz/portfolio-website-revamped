My Process

I'm dedicated to presenting clients with the best possible version of every project. Here's how I ensure success:

1

Meeting

We'll set up a time to talk about your project. During this phase we'll discuss your goals, your target audience, design, and budget.

2

Proposal

After our meeting, you'll receive a formal proposal via e-mail. It'll outline the services you can expect, the timeline, and price.

3

Coding

Next, I'll start the design and coding process. I'll test your site across major devices. You'll receive timely updates.

4

Delivery

Upon final approval, your website will go live. You'll receive all files and assets, plus control over the domain.
